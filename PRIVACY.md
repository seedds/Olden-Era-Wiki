# Privacy Policy — Olden Era Wiki

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Olden Era Wiki ("the app") is a free, unofficial companion reference app. **The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

**No data collection.** No analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no network requests for your data. All game content is bundled inside the app and works fully offline.

**On-device settings.** Preferences such as font size are stored locally using standard OS storage and never leave your device. Removing the app deletes them.

**Links.** The app may open your email client if you choose to contact support. Nothing is sent automatically.

**Children's privacy.** The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

**Contact.** seedds@gmail.com

**Changes.** Updates to this policy will be posted at this URL.
